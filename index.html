My Friend Cowboy Album Release

Recorded in his hometown of Oregon City, “From the West” is MFC’s debut LP and is written, performed, recorded, mixed and mastered by Adam, “From the West” showcases his love for The Beatles, tape, and songwriting as an art form. It wriggles with earworms and pulses with dusty drum machines. “From the West” is Blushing Grinning’s third release, featuring two unreleased bonus songs (Silly Little Lie, My Fake Cowboy) and packaged in one-of-a-kind handmade cassette plushy.
