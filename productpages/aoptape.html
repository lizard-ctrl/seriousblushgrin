Ode to Life - Age of Peace Tape

Cassette Tape

As the strings of hope are plucked in folk fashion, the “Ode to Life” is born unto the world as the debut LP from Age of Peace, the youngest love child of Angie Willcutt and Micah Wu (members of Artificial Go and ROD). Willcutt’s playful, yet transcendental lyrics dance freely over Wu’s explorative compositions, driven by the hypnotic pulse of mallet contacting drum. Their songs, bound to tape, command self documented messages pertaining to life and death, sharing the whisper of time.

The Age of Peace is now!
